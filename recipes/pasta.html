Pasta & Meatballs Recipe

This classic pasta and meatballs recipe is a comforting and delicious meal that is perfect for any occasion. Here is a simple recipe to make it at home.

Ingredients:

  • 500g spaghetti or your favorite pasta
  • 500g ground beef or a mix of beef and pork
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • Optional: extra Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until well done. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, parsley, beaten egg, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  3. Form the mixture into meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter.
  4. In a large skillet, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the meatballs and cook until browned on all sides and cooked through, about 10-12 minutes. Remove the meatballs from the skillet and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add the marinara sauce and bring to a simmer. Return the meatballs to the skillet and cook for an additional 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  6. Serve the meatballs and sauce over the cooked pasta. Garnish with extra Parmesan cheese if desired. Enjoy your homemade pasta and meatballs!
