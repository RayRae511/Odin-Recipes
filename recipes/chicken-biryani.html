Chicken Biryani Recipe

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic Indian dish made with basmati rice, tender chicken, and a blend of spices. Here is a simple recipe to make it at home.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Note: You can adjust the spice levels according to your preference. Serve the biryani with raita or a side salad for a complete meal.

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. In a large pot, heat some oil over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown. Remove half of the onions and set aside for garnishing.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste to the pot and sauté for a minute. Then add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  5. Add chicken pieces, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook until the chicken is browned on all sides.
  6. Add yogurt to the chicken mixture and cook for a few more minutes until the chicken is cooked through.
  7. In a separate pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil. Add the soaked and drained rice to the boiling water and cook until the rice is 70% cooked. Drain the rice.
  8. Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken in the pot. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  9. Once done, gently fluff the rice and garnish with the reserved fried onions and fresh coriander leaves.
  10. Serve the Chicken Biryani hot with raita or a side salad. Enjoy your homemade Chicken Biryani!
